About Diatom Impact

Diatom Impact is the impact investment and sustainability arm of Platform Capital, dedicated to transforming the African narrative through innovative, measurable, and lasting change. We drive projects that address key societal challenges, foster economic empowerment, and create opportunities for communities across Africa.

Our approach focuses on five critical areas of impact — Education, Health, Environment, Entrepreneurship & Innovation, and Economic Development. Through strategic partnerships, data-driven solutions, and a commitment to measurable results, we have touched the lives of hundreds of thousands, leaving a legacy of hope and opportunity.

Our Areas of Focus

Driving sustainable change across Africa through five transformative pillars.

Education

Closing Africa’s education gap through scholarships, modern learning spaces, literacy programs, and digital initiatives.

Health

Expanding healthcare access via clinics, clean water projects, medical outreach, and critical health partnerships.

Entrepreneurship

Empowering African innovators, MSMEs, and startups with mentorship, incubation, and funding access.

Gender Equality

Supporting women and girls in leadership, political participation, and economic inclusion.

Quality of Life

Providing housing, social safety nets, and restoring dignity for vulnerable communities.
